Meet Dennis

A calm, encouraging teacher with a student-first mindset

Dennis founded The KeySpace Piano Studio to create a space where students can build real skill while still enjoying the process of learning music.

His lessons balance strong fundamentals with flexibility, so each student can move forward with confidence whether they are brand new to piano, returning after time away, or working toward performance goals.

Lessons adapted to each student’s pace, goals, and musical interests
A strong focus on reading music, rhythm, and healthy technique
An encouraging environment that helps students feel capable from the start
